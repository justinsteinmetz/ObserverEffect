# ObserverEffect

A 45-minute grammar review lesson for 8B built around the observer effect — the phenomenon in which observation changes the behaviour of the observed.

Covers passive voice with 'by', passive question formation, and conjunction discrimination in preparation for Klassenarbeit 04.

## Files

**`index.html`** — Student tool. Post this URL in Teams. Firebase-gated: sections unlock from the teacher panel. Students work through passive voice tasks and a surveillance notice conjunction exercise, then submit a final sentence to the projector.

**`slides.html`** — Projector slides. Open fullscreen on the classroom computer before the lesson. Advance with arrow keys, spacebar, or click. Projected student sentences appear as an overlay in real time.

**`teacher.html`** — Teacher panel. Open on your phone. Three unlock buttons control what students can access. Incoming final sentences appear as cards — tap Project to send any sentence to the slides instantly.

## Operation

1. Open `teacher.html` on your phone
2. Open `slides.html` fullscreen on the projector
3. Post `justinsteinmetz.github.io/ObserverEffect` in Teams
4. Tap **Unlock Screen 0** when ready to begin
5. Tap **Unlock Part One** after Slide 06
6. Tap **Unlock Part Two** after Slide 10
7. Tap **Project** next to incoming sentences at the close
8. Tap **Reset session** after the lesson
